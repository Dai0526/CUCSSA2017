The inauguration of the Columbia China Prospects Conference is happening in March of 2017, aims to cultivate a year-long-discourse in a single program. As one of largest and most well-received China-focused events at Columbia University, the Conference brings together distinguished leaders from multiple fields and industries, government sectors, and academia who have unparalleled experience with China thus brings an audience from the whole Northeast region of the Untied States.
