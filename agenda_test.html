day 1 agenda

monday, june 22, 2015 - PRE-SUMMIT WORKSHOPS

8:00AM

Registration & Continental Breakfast 2

8:00AM - 9:00AM

Continental breakfast features locally sourced yogurt, granola, fruit and hard boiled eggs.

9:00AM

Conference Opening

9:00AM - 9:45AM

In this 3-hour long, intensive workshop, best-selling author will help you master the most underutilized marketing tool on the planet.

Learn how clever companies trade-in the hopes of a viral sensation for the certainty of sales success.

Create your own predictive models based on external forces in the market place. Learn how to manipulate the demand for the products and services you sell.

10:00AM

Case Study Presentation from Youtube

10:00AM - 12:00PM

Regina Hamilton – GM

There will be a case study presentation using YouTube videos. Are you ready to tap a trend?

Bring your laptop and an excel spreadsheet, because you’re about to become a content marketing ROI rockstar.

12:00PM

lunch with asian buffet

12:00PM - 01:00PM

You will have a chance to enjoy lunch with Asian Buffet. There are many types of food for your lunch. You feel free to enjoy buffet with your partners. Especially, you are going to have ice-cream, small cakes or fruits to finish your meal off.

01:00PM

Fast Track Designation for responsive Devices

01:00PM - 02:45PM

Diane Mcfarland – Executive Director

Flypaper Systems

Fast track is a process designed to facilitate the development, and expedite the review of products to treat serious conditions and fill customers’ need. The purpose is to get important new feature of product to fit all devices. Fast Track addresses a broad range of serious conditions

03:00PM

Global Trial Placement Strategies

03:00PM - 05:00PM

Marcus Young Garcia – CEO

Black Destination Systems

The meeting is to give strategies as trial version on over the world. This helps the organization can choose right countries for new plan. All products can take to trial countries for using. Then, we can find out which country the most suitable is?

05:00PM

closing of conference day

05:00PM - 05:30PM

Everyone in meeting will summaries ideas which gave during the conference. The closing of conference is the end. The secretary writes all things to manager and process in future
